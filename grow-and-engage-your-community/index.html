FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What are your community engagement solutions?

Nemeos community engagement solutions are made to ease NFT exchanges and usage for users. It covers mainly two products : (i) try before you buy, allowing new users to try the asset before buying it, and (ii) pay-per-use, a collateral-free rental service which helps unlock new usages.

What are the integration costs?

There are no integration costs.

Is the integration process complex?

The integration is made as easy as possible through our SDK. Contact us to discuss the integration procedure.

Is there a credit check for customers?

Nemeos is a DeFi protocol, meaning that the service is accessible to all users. Contrary to existing web2 solutions, there is no need for credit check.

What NFT standards do you support?

Today we mostly support ERC-721 and ERC-1155. Integrating another standard is not necessarily a heavy process, contact us if you use others NFT standards.
